Highlight ISML tags (`<isif>`, `<isloop>`, `<isprint>`, `<isscript>`, etc.) distinctly from plain HTML tags so ISML directives are easier to spot at a glance. Controlled by the `b2c-dx.isml.highlightTags` setting (on by default); disable it to render ISML tags in the normal HTML tag color. The color follows the active theme and never modifies your settings.
